Help & Rules

LIC India - Lapsed Policy Revival

A policy lapses if premiums are not paid within the grace period (15 days for monthly, 30 days for quarterly/half-yearly/yearly). To revive a lapsed policy, submit a written request, provide evidence of good health, and pay all arrears with interest. Use Form No. 680 or 720, available at licindia.in. Contact your nearest LIC branch for assistance.

[](https://licindia.in/faqs)

LIC India - Claims Procedure

For death claims, submit Claim Form 'A' (Form No. 3783) or 3783A (if policy ran for 3+ years), a certified death register extract, and the original policy document. For maturity claims, submit Form No. 3825 one month before the due date. Download forms from licindia.in.

[](https://licindia.in/download-forms)
